Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach


Muzhappilangad Drive-in Beach is a beach on the Malabar Coast in the district of Kannur in the state of Kerala in southwestern India. It is considered as the longest drive-in beach in Asia and is featured among the top 6 best beaches for driving in the world in BBC article for Autos.


Madikeri Hill Station


While talking about the best hill stations near Kannur, Madikeri will make a place on the top of the list. Once you are here, you will be amazed by the lush green hills and the valleys and that will soothe your urban eyes. Besides its picturesque beauty, this place is also famous for its ethereal connection. As this place is the home of the holy river, Kaveri, people come here for pilgrimage. The majestic beauty of the waterfalls and the virgin hilly region will mesmerize you instantly.
